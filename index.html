About Josie Zhuo

Josie Zhuo comes from a family with a long-standing tradition of practicing Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) and Tai Chi/Qi Gong. She is a licensed acupuncturist and holds a Master's degree in TCM from the Northwest Institute of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine. Josie honed her skills by apprenticing under renowned Chinese medical doctors, Dr. Xie Sun Shen and Dr. Tan Cun Sun.

With a unique approach to acupuncture and herbal therapy, Josie combines her knowledge of TCM with healing elements of Tai Chi and Qi Gong. She not only works to eliminate pain and illnesses but also helps patients to achieve overall wellness. She is dedicated to strengthening the flow of Qi energy and emphasizes the connection between mind and body. Josie encourages her patients to adopt preventive and self-healing techniques such as food therapy, self-acupressure, self-massage, meditation, and energy healing exercises.

Josie's patients and students appreciate her vast reservoir of TCM expertise, her life experiences, her compassionate care, and her commitment to the art of energy healing. She is a true master of her craft and continues to positively impact the lives of those she works with.
